How to use:

  1. Make sure your model files (metadata.json, model.json, weights.bin) are in a folder called "model"
  2. Click "Start Detection" to enable your webcam
  3. Perform sign language gestures in front of your camera
  4. View the prediction results in real-time
  5. Click "Stop Detection" when finished
